About Us

Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum is an officially recognised charity with the goal of preserving and encouraging people to learn the Slovak language and culture. While our main aim is supporting Slovak speaking families and children, anyone is welcome should they be interested in learning the Slovak language and traditions.

Our mission at Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum goes above just teaching the Slovak language, instead we aim to inform people of our heritage, and ensure that children with Slovak roots don't become out of touch with their own culture.
