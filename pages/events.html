Events

Easter Workshop (6th April). Step into the world of Slovak Easter traditions at our Easter Workshop, where culture, creativity, and community come together for a fun and welcoming afternoon. This hands-on event will offer something for everyone—families, individuals, and anyone who enjoys arts, crafts, and festive traditions. Join us in decorating eggs using traditional Slovak techniques, creating beautiful Easter crafts, and learning about the unique customs that make Slovak Easter so special. But this event is more than just a workshop—it's also a social gathering where you can meet new people, enjoy great company, and experience a warm community atmosphere. Whether you're coming with friends, family, or on your own, you’ll find a friendly, relaxed environment where connections happen naturally over delicious Slovak food and drinks. To make the afternoon even more special, we will have music playing throughout the event and, if all goes to plan, we hope to welcome a special guest. Come along, get creative, and celebrate Slovak Easter traditions in a fun and social setting! More details on our socials.




Kids Disco 2025 (12th July). A high-energy event to celebrate the end of the school year with music, dancing, and lots of fun! This special edition of our Kids Disco will also include a "Vysvedčenie" ceremony, where children will receive their end-of-year certificates and prizes to recognize their hard work and achievements. With a DJ bringing the party atmosphere, children can enjoy dancing, games, and entertainment while parents and other guests have the chance to relax, socialize, and celebrate together. Whether you're bringing your child or just stopping by to enjoy the event, it's a great way to connect with the community, mark the end of another fantastic year, and kick off the summer in style. Save the dates – more details coming soon!
